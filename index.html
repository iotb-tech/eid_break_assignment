Tips for Success
🚫

Don't use AI for reflection questions. We can tell. Answer from your own experience and in your own voice.

📅

Commit code daily. Don't wait until the last day. Your commit history is part of your grade.

🧩

Break problems down. If something feels overwhelming, split it into the smallest possible piece and solve that first.

🤝

Ask for help early. Post in the group chat when you're stuck. And help others — explaining code is the fastest way to understand it.

🔍

Read error messages. They usually tell you exactly what's wrong and on which line. Don't skip past them.

🪲

Use console.log strategically. It's your best debugging tool. Log early, log often, log meaningfully.

🧪

Test as you build. Don't write 500 lines then test. Check every 10–20 lines. Small loops catch small bugs.
